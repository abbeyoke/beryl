Microsoft Sharepoint 2010

Essential skills for all staff to improve collaboration

Mastering Microsoft Sharepoint 2010

Mar 25 • 9:00am

Training Description

SharePoint 2010 technologies provide a unified business collaboration platform for organizations, allowing for streamlined document management, simplified organization-wide access to information, and the ability to deploy customized solutions quickly and securely. In this course, you gain the skills to create, manage and customize SharePoint 2010 across your organization.

Course Outline

You will learn;

  • Overview of SharePoint 2010
  • Organizing Information with SharePoint Sites
  • Customizing your site
  • Effectively Managing and Securing Data
  • Storing information (List, Libraries)
  • Retrieving relevant information
  • Ensuring information integrity: site management plan, Content permission
  • Facilitating Offline Collaboration: Fostering team communication
  • Establishing a business process with workflows
  • Connecting SharePoint communities: Interacting with social networks through My Sites
  • Extending SharePoint with Office 2010: Increasing business productivity
  • Aggregating SharePoint schedules, contacts, documents and discussions in Outlook
  • Consolidating data reporting
  • Tailoring Websites: Automating business processes with workflows
  • Displaying dynamic data
  • Adapting Features for Custom Business Solutions: Maximizing "out-of-the-box" tools
  • Accessing web-based components
  • Building Line-of-Business (LOB) applications

Who should participate

This course is valuable for those managing, developing or working in a collaborative environment who wants to benefit from SharePoint 2010 technologies.

Training Details

Prerequisites

Basic Knowledge of Microsoft Office

Suitable for

Anyone managing, developing or working in a collaborative environment

Duration

5 days

Career Prospect

Microsoft Sharepoint User and Administrator
