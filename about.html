About Us

Beryl Consulting Limited is an integrated training, consulting and technological service company committed to the development of human capital. Beryl focuses on integrated management consulting and skill acquisition training services. The company is led by some of the top performers from industry and professional services organization. With over 15-20years average experience, our firm brings seasoned professionals to each project, ensuring effective and efficient implementation.

Beryl Consulting Limited's flexible structure as well as the use of competent methodologies and internationally applied practices enable us to adjust to the ever dynamic market needs thus ensure direct and quality results. Our services cover all aspect of project's life cycle: from the conception of the idea to the planning, Implementation, delivery and continuous maintenance using appropriate implementation and quality assurance methodologies.

Currently, Beryl Consulting Limited is primarily active in the field of Business and technology Consulting, Training and end to end business solutions with emphasis on Performance Management and Information Technology.

Our Core Values

  • We offer clients reduced total cost of ownership by providing exceptional value at competitive rates.
  • We build to suit, taking into consideration the peculiarities of our clients' environment to deliver appropriate solutions.
  • Enhance customer satisfaction, by leveraging our deep experience, we bring proven methodologies.

Vision

It is our dream to be the pre-eminent trusted advisor and change agent to leading businesses, institutions and governments in Africa and emerging markets.

Mission

We seek to be the strategic empowerment partner enable our clients excel.

We are committed to the following:

  • Be partner of choice to our clients providing them innovative, creative and cost effective solutions that enable their capacity and grants them the competitive advantage.
  • Help transform the businesses of our clients by providing strategic insights that help them in their performance journey.
  • The return to True North principles as unchanging platforms for results, for we are concerned about lasting impact; as our clients through our relationship with them, have answers for today’s challenges and capacity for the unknown future.
  • A place of opportunities, fulfilment and growth for our staff and our owners.
  • By the grace of God, to make a decent profit by delivery of our services.
